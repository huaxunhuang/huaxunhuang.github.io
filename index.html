Short Bio

I am an assistant professor in School of Informatics (National Characteristic Demonstration Software School), Xiamen University. Prior to that, I received my PhD degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2023, supervised by Prof. Shing-Chi Cheung. My current research topic is studying API evolution in Android applications. I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering, Northeastern University on June 2017. You are welcome to contact with me through huanghuaxun at xmu dot edu dot cn.
