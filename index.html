Short Bio

I am a PhD student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology starting from Fall 2017, supervised by Prof. Shing-Chi Cheung. My research includes but not limited to program analysis, software maintenance. My current research topic is studying API evolution in Android applications. I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Software College, Northeastern University in June 2017. You are welcome to contact with me through hhuangas at cse dot ust dot hk.
